Atropos Atropos is the eldest of the three sisters, responsible for cutting the thread of life and determining the end of a person's life.

Myths About Atropos

  • Cutting the Thread of Life: Atropos was responsible for cutting the thread of life spun by Clotho and measured by Lachesis. Her role was to decide when a person’s life would end.
  • The Inevitable End: Atropos’ decision was final and irreversible, symbolizing the inevitability of death and the end of life’s journey.
  • Role in the Fate Trio: While Clotho spun the thread and Lachesis measured it, Atropos had the crucial task of cutting it, marking the end of a person’s life.
  • Symbolic Depictions: In art, Atropos is often depicted with a pair of shears or a dagger, representing her role in cutting the thread of life.
