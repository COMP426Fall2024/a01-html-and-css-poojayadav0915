Lachesis Lachesis is the second sister of the three Fates, responsible for measuring the thread of life and determining the length of one's life span.

Myths About Lachesis

  • Measuring the Thread of Life: Lachesis was responsible for measuring the length of the thread of life spun by Clotho. She determined the fate of individuals by setting the length of their life span.
  • Determiner of Fate: Lachesis had the power to influence and determine the course of a personâ€™s life, deciding their destiny and ensuring that their allotted time was fulfilled.
  • Role in the Fate Trio: While Clotho spun the thread and Atropos cut it, Lachesis' role was critical in measuring and determining how long each person's life would be.
  • Symbolic Depictions: In art, Lachesis is often depicted with a rod or a measuring tape, symbolizing her role in measuring out the thread of life.
